Our Story

Our journey began with extensive research across various areas to identify the best location for quality IT education. After careful evaluation, we chose Nariyawal as our base to serve the community with accessible tech training.

On May 13, 2024, three passionate partners came together with a shared vision. Each contributing ₹20,000, we established Bytecore Computer Centre with a total investment of ₹60,000.

Today, we're proud to provide quality computer education to students in our community, helping them build successful careers in technology. Our focus remains on practical, hands-on training that prepares students for real-world opportunities.
