Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our courses and enrollment process

Eligibility varies by course. For beginner courses like CCC and Basic Programming, no prior experience is required. For advanced courses like Full Stack Development or Data Science, we recommend basic computer knowledge. O Level requires 10+2 qualification. Please check individual course pages for specific requirements.

Yes, we have a dedicated placement cell that works with 500+ hiring partners across India. We provide resume building, interview preparation, and direct placement opportunities. Many of our courses have 100% placement records, especially in-demand programs like Full Stack Development and Data Science.

ADCA (Advanced Diploma in Computer Applications) is a 1-year comprehensive course covering programming, accounting, DTP and office applications. MDCA (Master Diploma in Computer Applications) is a more advanced 15-month program that includes additional modules like advanced programming, database management, networking and web technologies. MDCA is ideal for those seeking deeper technical knowledge.

Yes, we offer flexible payment options for most courses. You can pay in 2-4 installments depending on the course duration and fee. Some bank financing options are also available. Please contact our admission counselors for customized payment plans.

NIELIT certificates (O Level, CCC etc.) are government-recognized and have lifetime validity. These certificates are accepted for government jobs, promotions and higher education across India. They don't require renewal and are considered permanent qualifications.
